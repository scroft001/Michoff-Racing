About Michael

Michael Michoff is 15 years old and has been racing go-karts for 8 years. He is a California native and lives in Roseville, a suburb of Sacramento. He is currently on summer break but does home schooling during the year in order to travel and follow his passion for racing. He will be a Sophomore in high school in the fall. In his free time he likes to play video games, mostly racing simulations like Forzia but will not turn away from cooperative games like Call of Duty or League of Legends. His ultimate goal is to become a professional driver and will do anything it takes to reach that mark.
